Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich





The amazingly simple treat that takes you back to your childhood!


Ingredients:


  • (2 pieces) Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Grape Jelly


Steps:


  1. Lay pieces of bread on a plate or flat surface
  2. Use a knife to spread a layer of peanut butter on one piece of bread
  3. Use knife to spread layer of grape jelly onto the other piece of bread
  4. Close sandwich together and cut into halves or triangles, or leave whole!
  5. Enjoy!
