Smores





An American classic treat, traditionally made on camping trips over a fire. But can be made at home just as well to be enjoyed anytime! Melted chocolate and marshmellows pressed between two graham crackers


Ingredients:


  • Graham Crackers
  • Marshmellows
  • Hershey's chocolate bars


Steps:


  1. Use a poker stick to roast a marshmellow over a heat source.
  2. When marshmellow is ready, take a graham cracker- break it in half
  3. Place piece of chocolate onto cracker, and smoosh melted marshmellow on top
  4. Close smore with graham cracker, and press togther
  5. Enjoy!
