Spaghetti





An italian dish of buttery noddles and tomato sauce.


Ingredients:


  • Angel Hair Pasta
  • Ragu Tomato sauce
  • 1 lb of hamburger meat


Steps:


  1. Boil pot with water and add box of Angel hair pasta
  2. Place hamburger meat into skillet and begin browning
  3. Add sauce to meat once it has cooked fully
  4. When pasta is ready romove from stove
  5. Grab a plate, add pasta, and cover with spaghetti sauce
  6. Enjoy!
